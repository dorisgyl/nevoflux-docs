## The short version

1. **Install NevoFlux** for your platform and launch it. The AI agent is bundled
   in — there is nothing else to install.
2. **Open the sidebar** with **`Ctrl+Shift+A`** (**`Cmd+Shift+A`** on macOS) and
   **configure an AI model** (this is required before the agent can do anything).
3. **Ask the agent** to do something — summarize the page, find information, fill a
   form, build a small app, or save a note to your knowledge base.
