NevoFlux is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Download

Grab the latest build from the [releases page](https://github.com/dorisgyl/nevoflux/releases/latest)
and pick the asset that matches your operating system and CPU architecture.

## Platform notes

- **Windows** — run the installer and follow the prompts.
- **macOS** — open the `.dmg` and drag NevoFlux to Applications.
- **Linux** — use the provided AppImage or distribution package.
